Loksatta

Loksatta is a Marathi daily newspaper in Maharashtra.It is published by The Indian Express Group and was launched on 14 January 1948.
Pudhari

Pudhari is a popular Marathi daily, printed in three centers[2] and distributed in Maharashtra, Goa and North Karnataka.
