Tamil News

Dina Thanthi

Dina Thanthi is a Tamil language daily newspaper. It was founded by S. P. Adithanar in Madurai in 1942.it is India's largest daily printed in the Tamil language.
Dinamalar

Dinamalar is a National Tamil daily newspaper. It was founded in 1951 by T. V. Ramasubba Iyer.[1] Dinamalar has an average circulation of 942,812.
Dinakaran

Dinakaran is a Tamil daily newspaper,It was founded by K. P. Kandasamy in 1977 and is currently owned by media conglomerate Sun Group's- Sun Network.
Theekkathir

Theekkathir is a Tamil newspaper ran by Toiling Masses Welfare Trust.Theekkathir is being published from Madurai,Chennai,Coimbatore and Tiruchirappalli.
Tamil Murasu

Tamil Murasu is a Singapore based Tamil language newspaper.Launched in 1935.Tamil Murasu is Singapore's only Tamil language newspaper
Hindu Tamil

Hindu Tamil Thisai is a Tamil daily newspaper headquartered at Chennai.It is published by The Hindu Group.
