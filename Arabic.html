Arabic NewsPapers

Al-Akhbar

Al Akhbar (Arabic: الأخبار‎; The News in English) is an Arabic daily newspaper based in Egypt. It is a state-owned semi-official newspaper.
Aljazeera

Al Jazeera is an international Arabic news channel based in Doha, Qatar that is operated by the media conglomerate Al Jazeera Media Network.
Ahram

Al-Ahram founded on 5 August 1875, is the most widely circulating Egyptian daily newspaper, and the second oldest after al-Waqa'i`al-Masriya.
Elheddaf

El Heddaf is an Algerian nationwide daily "newspaper" allegedly devoted to football.Using their trademark "You make the News" style.
Alqabas

Al-Qabas was launched on 22 February 1972.The founding shareholders of Al Qabas,according to the founding contract as printed in the official gazette.
Albayan

Al Bayan is a popular Arabic language newspaper in the United Arab Emirates.The paper is based in Dubai.
